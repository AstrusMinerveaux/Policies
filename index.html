Refunds & Exchanges Policy

Effective Date: May 21, 2025

At The Astrus Press, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Refunds & Exchanges Policy outlines our procedures for returns, refunds, and exchanges.

1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

All shoppers are entitled to a 30-day money-back guarantee to ensure they are fully satisfied with their product. This guarantee applies to all purchases made through https://astrus.live and associated platforms.

2. Digital Products

For digital products (software, e-books, digital downloads, etc.):

  • No physical return is necessary as only a refund is required
  • Refund requests must be made within 30 days of purchase
  • Digital products cannot be exchanged, only refunded
  • Access to digital products will be revoked upon processing of the refund

3. Physical Products

For physical products:

  • Products must be returned within 14 days of requesting a return to be eligible for a refund
  • Products must be returned in the same condition in which they were received
  • Original packaging and all accessories must be included
  • Refunds will be processed 24 to 48 hours after we receive the returned product

4. Return Shipping Costs

The cost of return shipping will be paid for by The Astrus Press. There is no expense on the consumer to return a product. We will provide you with a prepaid return shipping label upon approval of your return request.

5. Exchanges

Product exchanges are available under the following conditions:

  • Only physical products can be exchanged
  • Digital products cannot be exchanged
  • Products can be exchanged for a similar priced product in the same category as the purchased product
  • Exchange requests must be made within 30 days of purchase
  • The original product must be returned in its original condition

6. How to Request a Return or Exchange

To request a return or exchange:

  1. Contact us at support@astrus.live or soutien@astrus.live
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return/exchange
  3. We will respond within 24 hours with instructions
  4. Follow the provided instructions to complete your return or exchange

7. Processing Times

  • Return requests are processed within 24 hours of receipt
  • Refunds are issued 24-48 hours after receiving returned physical products
  • Digital product refunds are processed within 24 hours of approval
  • Refunds will appear on your original payment method within 3-5 business days

8. Non-Refundable Items

Certain items may not be eligible for return or refund, including:

  • Personalized or customized products
  • Products damaged by misuse or normal wear
  • Products returned after the 30-day period
